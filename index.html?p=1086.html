Payments for High risk merchants

A high risk merchant account is a payment processing account for businesses that are classified as high risk services by banks,such as airplane tickets, online services, gaming, crypto currencies, insurance products, etc.  

Seamless integrations from our payment gateway directly to your website makes accepting debit and credit cards online easier than ever. Certitrade smooth payment gateway helps you  to connect in the digital payment processing world.

The key features are:

  • Embedded payment sheet
  • Multicurrency and main languages support
  • Card payments (3D/No secure)
  • One Click payments
  • Recurring Payments for subscriptions and services
  • Payouts
  • Dashboard for reconciliation and reporting

We help high risk merchants to find right payment methods for their site. Contact us and we tell You more.
